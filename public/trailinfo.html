Mt Olympus

Head out on this 7.8-mile out-and-back trail near Cottonwood Heights, Utah. Generally considered a challenging route. This is a very popular area for hiking, so you'll likely encounter other people while exploring. The best times to visit this trail are March through October. Dogs are welcome and may be off-leash in some areas.
